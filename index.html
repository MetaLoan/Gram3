Telegram Reimagined with Web3 Integration

OnlyGram is a next-generation Telegram client that seamlessly integrates Web3 wallets and modular miniapps, bringing the power of decentralized applications to your messaging experience.

Key Features

Discover how OnlyGram enhances your Telegram experience with powerful Web3 capabilities

Integrated Web3 Wallet

Manage your crypto assets, NFTs, and tokens directly within your messaging app. Send and receive crypto as easily as sending a message.

Modular MiniApps

Extend functionality with a growing ecosystem of decentralized applications that run directly in your chat interface.

Enhanced Privacy

End-to-end encryption combined with blockchain technology ensures your communications and transactions remain secure and private.

DeFi Integration

Access decentralized exchanges, lending platforms, and yield farming opportunities without leaving your messaging environment.

Cross-Chain Support

Interact with multiple blockchains including Ethereum, Solana, Binance Smart Chain, and more from a single interface.

Decentralized Identity

Manage your digital identity across Web3 applications with integrated support for ENS domains and other decentralized identity solutions.
