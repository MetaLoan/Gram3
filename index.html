Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our energy rental service? Find answers to the most common questions below.

What is TRON energy and why do I need it?

+

TRON energy is a resource used to pay for smart contract executions and transactions on the TRON blockchain. When you execute a smart contract or interact with a dApp, you need energy to cover the computational costs. Without energy, these operations would consume your TRX tokens directly, which is often more expensive.

How much can I save by renting energy?

+

By renting energy instead of directly spending TRX on transaction fees, you can save up to 90% on your transaction costs. The exact savings depend on the current network conditions and transaction complexity, but most users see significant cost reductions.

How long does the energy rental last?

+

Our energy rental is transaction-based, not time-based. This means you're purchasing energy for a specific number of transactions, and it remains available until you use it up. There's no expiration date, so you can use the energy whenever you need it.

How quickly will I receive the energy after payment?

+

Energy is delivered instantly to your wallet address as soon as your payment is confirmed on the blockchain. This typically takes less than a minute. Our automated system monitors incoming transactions and processes energy transfers immediately.

Is there a minimum or maximum amount of energy I can rent?

+

You can rent energy for as little as 1 transaction or as many as 10,000 transactions in a single order. For larger requirements, please contact our support team, and we can arrange a custom solution for your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We currently accept TRX and USDT-TRC20 as payment methods. When paying with USDT, our system automatically converts it to TRX at the current market rate before processing your energy rental.
